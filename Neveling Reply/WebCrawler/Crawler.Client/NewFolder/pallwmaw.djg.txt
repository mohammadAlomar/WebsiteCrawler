<h1>Welcome</h1>
<p> </p>
<p>We are BBC Studios TalentWorks. We identify and develop emerging talent who are interested in creating new show ideas with BBC Studios.</p>
<p>Whether it’s working with YouTube stars Dan Howell and Phil Lester, launching <em>Di’s Salon</em> for Radio 1, or co-producing <em>PRESSED</em> for Radio 1Xtra, our team partners with some of the most exciting talent around.</p>
<p>Across a year, we produce and host Creator Residencies, presenter training days, retreats and much more to help provide new talent with the skills to reach their potential.</p>
<p>Previous alumni include Basma Khalifa, who worked with Louis Theroux’s indie production company Mindhouse, Liv Little, founder of gal-dem, who was a writer in residence at BBC Studios creating an original pilot script, and Ajay Tegala, who finished his placement with BBC Studios Natural History Unit presenting a segment on BBC Two’s <em>Springwatch</em>.</p>
<p>After a successful residency with the makers of the multi-award winning <em>I May Destroy You</em>, Various Artists Limited, comedian Charlie George had her script optioned by the company.</p>
<p>Find out more about placements past and present on the Creator Residencies page.</p>
